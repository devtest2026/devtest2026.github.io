What types of projects do you specialize in?

We specialize in Full-Stack Web (PHP, Laravel, MERN, Next.js, Django), Mobile Apps (React Native, Flutter, iOS, Android), AI/ML solutions, Blockchain/Web3, and intelligent automation bots. We love projects that blend multiple disciplines.

How long does a typical project take?

A mobile app typically takes 6-12 weeks. A full-stack web platform takes 4-16 weeks depending on complexity. AI/ML integrations vary from 2 weeks (API integration) to 3+ months (custom model training). We provide a detailed timeline after discovery.

Do you build cross-platform or native mobile apps?

Both. We build cross-platform apps with React Native and Flutter (one codebase, iOS + Android), and native apps with Swift (iOS) and Kotlin (Android) when maximum performance or platform-specific features are required.

Can you integrate AI/ML into an existing app?

Absolutely. We can add AI features to any existing platform — chatbots via OpenAI APIs, recommendation engines, predictive analytics, computer vision with OpenCV, or custom ML models with TensorFlow/PyTorch.

Do you sign NDAs before discussions?

Absolutely. We sign NDAs before any project discussion. Your ideas, plans, and technical requirements are treated with complete confidentiality. Fixed-price contracts are available so there are no surprises.

What is your pricing model?

We offer both fixed-price and time-and-materials contracts. After a free discovery call, we provide a detailed proposal with clear milestones and payment schedules. No hidden fees, ever.
