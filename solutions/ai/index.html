Our Process

From Data to Deployed Alpha

We follow a rigorous research-to-production pipeline that ensures every AI model is thoroughly validated before it trades real capital.

01

Data Pipeline Engineering

Clean, normalized tick data from 50+ exchanges with on-chain data feeds, alternative data sources, and real-time streaming infrastructure via Kafka.

02

Quantitative Research

Hypothesis-driven alpha research with rigorous statistical testing, overfitting prevention via cross-validation, and out-of-sample validation on held-out data.

03

Paper Trading Validation

Every strategy runs in paper trading mode for 30+ days before live deployment to validate real-world performance matches backtests under live market conditions.

04

Live Monitoring & Iteration

Real-time dashboards tracking PnL, Sharpe ratio, drawdown, and model drift — with automated alerts, kill switches, and continuous model retraining.
